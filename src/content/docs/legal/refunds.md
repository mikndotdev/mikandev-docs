# General Refund Policy
- We generally do not accept refunds or cancellations unless there are special circumstances. All products are digital and delivered immediately upon payment confirmation.
- All products have a trial period or free version available. Please try them before making a purchase.
- If you encounter any issues with the product, please contact us through our support channels. We will do our best to assist you.

# Exceptions
- In cases where there is a defect in the product or if the product does not function as advertised, we may consider a refund upon review.
- Refund requests must be made within 14 days of purchase and must include a valid reason.
- Refunds will be processed using the same payment method used for the purchase.
